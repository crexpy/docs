Installation

  1. Download the Loader with the link in your purchase order

  2. Create your account

  3. Head to the 'Licenses' tab and redeem your key

  4. Go back to the 'Home' tab

  5. Select your product (cheat or unlocker) and click on Load

  6. After injecting one or both products, start your game
