Installation

  1. Download the Loader with the link in your purchase order

  2. Extract the .rar file and run the Loader as Administrator

  3. Accept the Discord authorization that opens in your web browser

  4. Enter the license key that you received after purchase

  5. Go to the 'Unlocker' tab

    1. In the 'Config' section, enable 'Bypass' (Should be enabled by default)
    2. Customize the rest of your settings
    3. Click 'Start' and press 'Yes' on the Fiddler root certificate showing up
    4. After accepting the Fiddler Root, start your game


    Your cookies will be detected automatically when you start your game
